Demo harness

Playground: type like in any textbox

Simulates a macOS textbox for deterministic testing. For real TextEdit/browser fields, use Manual focused capture or the tray shortcut. Completing a fifth word freezes that chunk and runs inference on it while you keep typing; when candidates arrive, the chunk is underlined to show which words they would replace. 1 through 5 or click picks a candidate, Tab accepts the highlighted one, Esc keeps your text, and ending a sentence (or a newline) closes the session.
