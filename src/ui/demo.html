Demo harness

Playground: type like in any textbox

Simulates a macOS textbox until Workstream 3's Accessibility observer lands. Candidates appear and refresh live above the caret while you type, like the macOS Pinyin IME: 1 through 5 or click picks a candidate, Tab accepts the highlighted one, Esc keeps your text, and ending a sentence (or a newline) closes the session. The model window is the trailing five words: older words become final as you keep typing.
