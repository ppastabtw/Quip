Simulates a macOS textbox for deterministic testing. For real TextEdit/browser fields, use Manual focused capture or the tray shortcut. Candidates appear and refresh live above the caret while you type, like the macOS Pinyin IME: 1 through 5 or click picks a candidate, Tab accepts the highlighted one, Esc keeps your text, and ending a sentence (or a newline) closes the session.
